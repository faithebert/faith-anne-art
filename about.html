About Me

Hi there! My name is Faith Anne and I am a Graphic Design student at Algonquin College, heading into my second year.

Skills

  • Realism + Graphite Pencil
  • Organization
  • Hard Work
  • Colour Selection
  • Design

I have been doing art since I was 3 years old. I love working with graphite pencil but have experimented in recent years with coloured pencil and a minimalist style that goes hand in hand with my budding knowledge of Adobe Software. I love my program at Algonquin College and thrive in typography and graphic design sections of the course. I have a pug named Frodo that means the world to me, and I spend most of my free time with my family and dog watching television.
